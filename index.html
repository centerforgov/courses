Performance Analytics: Tools and Techniques

A GovEx Online Course


Find Out More Apply now

Developed and taught by experienced practitioners from GovEx, this online course is designed to equip local government employees with the necessary knowledge and tools to understand, promote, and implement open data and performance analytics programs in their organization.


The course consists of eight weekly sessions beginning February 22, 2016. The topics include:


Session 1: Overview of Open Data & Performance Analytics
Session 2: Creating Compelling Forums for Decision Making
Session 3: Culture Change
Session 4: Applying your Framework to Real World Problems
Session 5: Stakeholder Engagement
Session 6: Data in Action - Examples from the Field (Part 1)
Session 7: Data in Action, Examples from the Field (Part 2)
Session 8: Putting It All Together

Click here to view the course syllabus.

The course begins February 22, 2016 and runs for 8 weeks.


Each week, participants will view a pre-recorded video and/or multimedia presentation, complete a learning activity, and contribute to an online discussion and/or participate on the weekly live virtual "office hours." Students will receive weekly written feedback on their exercises and have multiple opportunities to interact with the instructors and their colleagues in the class.


Upon successful completion of the course participants will receive a Johns Hopkins University Certificate of Completion.
